1.1 The value of Persona & journey map

To create a reliable and realistic representations of your key audience segments is the fundemental research for product planning. The importance of persona is like electricity and gas towards the product design and development progress afterwards. The persona and customer journey map work takes 1 or 2 UX researcher three month (about RMB 10,000~30,000) for PeX project. The granularity and precision will largely impact the success of the product in many ways like information architecture, the technical solution selection(commission & depolyment), and MKT operation based on the competitive analysis.
